## Why this fits

The MedBud Research & Science section already has threads on terpenes (S.O.H™'s chart, 6 Apr) and irradiation effects on THC reporting (Grumbleweed). Phenologue's methodology is built around exactly those concerns: chemotype-first analysis, batch-level fidelity, irradiation status as a first-class field, organoleptic inference for unmeasured terpenes. The community is already asking the questions Phenologue is built to answer, and the code is open so any contribution is auditable.

I'm not pitching a product — Phenologue is free and will stay free. I'm pitching a structured way for MedBud patients to contribute to a dataset that benefits everyone, alongside whatever moderation / writing / tooling help is most useful.
